# 05 Non Goals

## Product Non-Goals (v1)
- Reader accounts or authentication
- Paywalls or subscriptions with gated content
- Social mechanics (likes, upvotes, thread battles)
- Popups or aggressive conversion UX
- Ad network integrations
- Social feed embeds in page body
- AI-generated essay summaries
- Heavy animation systems or parallax
- Complex dashboards beyond CMS/editorial tasks
- Dark mode toggle

## Technical Non-Goals (v1)
- SPA architecture for core reading pages
- Multi-service microservices setup
- Custom video/audio hosting pipeline
- Advanced recommendation engine

## Reasoning
These exclusions protect reading quality, keep build scope realistic for `2-4 weeks`, and preserve low operational cost.
