# 00 Brief

## Project
A personal studio/lab website that curates essays, mixed-media projects, idea outlines, photography, and embedded media.

## Positioning
- Canonical publishing home
- Calm, editorial, museum-of-ideas tone
- External platforms used for distribution only

## Primary Visitor Actions
1. Read essays
2. Explore projects
3. Subscribe for updates

## Audience
- Primary: AI/tech builders, creative peers (writers/artists/designers)
- Secondary: curious readers, collaborators, future clients/employers

## Success Criteria (First 3 Months)
- Polished site quality suitable for sharing publicly
- Stable publishing workflow from CMS to live site
- Early subscriber growth and initial readership circulation

## Constraints
- Fast and lightweight
- Maintainable long-term
- Minimal dependencies
- Monthly operations under `$25`
- v1 launch in `2-4 weeks`
